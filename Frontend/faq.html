Frequently Asked Questions

What is Samnwadh?

Samnwadh is an open-source platform designed to facilitate real-time collaboration, messaging, and community building.

Is Samnwadh free to use?

Yes! Samnwadh is completely free and open-source. You can contribute to its development on GitHub.

How do I contribute to Samnwadh?

You can contribute by visiting our Contribute section and following the guidelines on our GitHub repository.

Is my data secure on Samnwadh?

Absolutely! We use end-to-end encryption to ensure your conversations remain private and secure.
